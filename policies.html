Shipping Policy

We ship across India from our Auto Veo Pitstop workshop. Most orders are packed and handed to our courier partner within 1–2 business days.

You'll get an SMS and email with tracking details once your order ships. Delivery times are estimates and can shift during festive periods or due to courier delays outside our control.

Cash on Delivery (COD) is available on orders under ₹5,000. A small COD handling fee of ₹29 applies at checkout.

Returns & Refunds

Not happy with something? You can request a return within 7 days of delivery.

Eligible for return

  • Item arrived damaged, defective, or different from what you ordered
  • Unused accessories and toys still in original packaging with tags/seals intact
  • Wrong size or fit on helmets and riding gear, unworn and with tags attached

Not eligible for return

  • Used or installed parts (e.g. fitted seat covers, opened lube/chemical kits)
  • Items without original packaging, accessories, or manuals
  • Products marked "Final Sale" at checkout

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5–7 business days. COD orders are refunded via bank transfer or UPI.

Warranty

Manufacturer warranty terms apply as stated on individual product pages — typically 6 months on electronics-based accessories (like phone mounts with charging) and 12 months on helmets against manufacturing defects. Die-cast toy models are covered against manufacturing defects for 30 days from delivery, but general wear from display or handling is not covered.

Privacy Policy

We collect only what we need to process your order and improve the shop: your name, email, phone number, delivery address, and order history. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google — we never receive or store your Google password.

How we use your information

  • To process orders, arrange delivery, and send order updates
  • To respond to support requests and returns
  • To send newsletter emails, only if you've subscribed — you can unsubscribe anytime

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your personal data to third parties
  • We don't store your full card details — payments are handled by our payment gateway partner, not on our own servers

You can request a copy of your data or ask us to delete your account by emailing our support team (see Contact below).
